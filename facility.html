Facilities

Laboratories

Laboratory is the most essential part of Science education. In this respect, our school has provision of separate physic, chemistry, biology and computer Science lab with new advanced equipment and computers.

Library

The school provides a separate library with text books, reference books and audio-visual items. The library also provides daily newspapers, national and international journals, magazines, DVDs.

Provision of Health

Pokhariya provides a facility of basic health service by a trained full time staff nurse during school time.

Transportation

Pokhariya Secondary School has own two new buses which provide transportation facility for the students who come to school from different parts of the city.
