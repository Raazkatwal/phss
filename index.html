Shree Pokhariya Secondary School!

Shree Pokhariya Secondary School which is situated in Biratnagar metropolitan city ward No.- 3, Morang is a community based school. It is fully a government funded school too. The school is located far from the main city of Biratnagar. It is about one kilometer Eastern part of Biratnagar Airport. Being a community based school, it has been well known institution as a quality education in Biratnagar as well as all over Province 1. The school has been the major educational institution for Janajatis, backward families, lower and middle class people as well as the Madheshi people of this region. If we observe the total number of students, the numbers of girls are more than the number of boys. In this way, it has been cleared that the majority of girls have been studying in community based schools.

Vision of Education

We, the teachers of Pokhariya higher Secondary School, aim at forming fearless and vibrant citizens, who are able to face the challenges of life.

Recogniztion

It is an international institution especially devoted to the education of youth. The institution is open to students of all communities, irrespective of status, caste, creed and religion.

Continuous Assessment

Marks are given for general day to day class work. These will be supplemented by occasional revisional tests. Promotion will be based partly on daily assessment and partly on class tests both having equal emphasis.

Learning By Doing

For the very start “Learning by Doing” is emphasized. They have Terminal Examination and continuous assessment is done for all subjects which will be graded and given to parents on the day of report card distribution.

Well Equipped Computer Lab

We have well equipped air-condition computer lab with 24 hours power backup facilities which helps to provide quality computer education to our students. Computer practical classes are available for all level students.

Vehicle Services

Reliable vehicle service is also available for our students. There are 2 buses for all students to arrive at school go home safely.
